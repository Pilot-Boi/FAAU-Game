CONTAINMENT PROCEDURES // TEST SUBJECT PROGRAM

All active test subjects are currently housed within Sublevel 3 restricted containment.

Due to extreme variation in subject behavior and capability sets, containment environments are customized on a per-subject basis.

Standard containment cells are modified according to observed subject abilities and behavioral risks.

The following specialized containment adaptations are currently in use:
- Reinforced containment structures
- Vocal restriction apparatus
- Observation barrier cells

Refer to individual containment protocol documents for further details.

NOTE:
Continuous monitoring of all subject containment areas is maintained through internal surveillance systems.

Unauthorized access to surveillance systems is prohibited under Facility Security Directives.
