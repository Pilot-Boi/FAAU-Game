CONTAINMENT ADDENDUM
OBSERVATION BARRIER CELLS

Standard barred containment cells are unsuitable
for certain subjects demonstrating significant
morphological flexibility.

Structural testing indicated that conventional
barred barriers present exploitable weaknesses
for subjects capable of altering body shape or
reducing physical profile.

To mitigate this risk, affected containment units
have been retrofitted with reinforced observation
barriers in place of traditional bars.

These barriers provide a continuous containment
surface while maintaining full visual monitoring
capability for research and security personnel.

This protocol is currently active for:

SUBJECT 004
SUBJECT 005

Personnel should note that subjects housed within
observation barrier cells may attempt to test the
integrity of barrier surfaces during periods of
heightened agitation.

All containment integrity alerts must be reported
immediately to Sublevel 3 security command.
