CONTAINMENT ADDENDUM
REINFORCED STRUCTURAL CELLS

Certain test subjects demonstrate physical strength or structural disruption capabilities that exceed standard containment tolerances.

These subjects are housed in reinforced containment cells constructed with high-density alloy supports and structural reinforcement layers.

This protocol is currently active for:
SUBJECT 003
SUBJECT 006

Routine inspection of containment integrity is required every 12 hours.

Personnel are advised not to approach reinforced cells without being accompanied by a member of security staff.
