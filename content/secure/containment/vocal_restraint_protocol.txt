CONTAINMENT ADDENDUM
VOCAL RESTRAINT PROTOCOL

Certain test subjects demonstrate the ability to
produce effects through spoken or projected vocal
output.

In these cases, a specialized vocal restraint
apparatus is required during containment.

The apparatus prevents the subject from fully
articulating speech while still allowing monitored
breathing and feeding access.

This protocol is currently active for:

SUBJECT 002
SUBJECT 007

Removal of the apparatus requires authorization from
Level 5 personnel.

All interactions with restrained subjects must occur
under full security supervision.
